About TOSHMONEY ?

We are humanitarian and financial mutual aid community; a person to person donation approach. We provide technical platform and support which helps millions of participants with the sole purpose of attaining financial stability.

Do I have to refer someone to earn?

There aren’t any obligations to bring someone before you earn. The system is designed to automatically pair members to you.

How Do I Receive My Payment?

Once you have donated and have been fully activated, you become eligible to receive your payment from 2 members either through bank payment or transfer.
